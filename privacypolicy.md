# zekbot420 - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

## 1. Overview
zekbot420 is an open-source Discord bot that provides music playback, video downloads, utility commands, and optional integrations such as Last.fm. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your choices.

## 2. Data We Collect

### a) Discord Data
When you use zekbot420, the bot may access:
- Your Discord user ID
- Server (guild) ID
- Channel ID
- Messages used to trigger commands
- Audio files you upload
- Logs of deleted messages and moderative actions (if you have modlogs setup)

This data is used strictly to operate commands and provide functionality. Your data is never sold or shared with third parties.

### b) Last.fm Integration
If you choose to link your Last.fm account, zekbot420 may store:
- Your Last.fm username
- Associated Discord user ID

zekbot420 does not store your Last.fm password or sensitive login credentials.

## 3. How We Use Data
Collected data is used to:
- Execute bot commands
- Provide music and utility features
- Link your Discord account with Last.fm (if enabled)
- Improve bot performance and reliability

## 4. Data Storage
- Data may be stored temporarily in memory or in a database
- Last.fm usernames may be stored persistently to maintain account linking
- Data is not sold or shared with third parties

## 5. Data Retention
- Temporary data is cleared automatically
- Persistent data (like Last.fm usernames) is stored until you request removal

## 6. Your Rights
You may:
- Request removal of your stored data
- Stop using the bot at any time
- Unlink your Last.fm account

To request data deletion, contact zekkie at zekkiewashere[at]icloud[dot]com.

## 7. Third-Party Services
zekbot420 uses the following third-party services:
- Discord (for bot functionality)
- Last.fm (for music stat tracking features)

Their respective privacy policies apply.

## 8. Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored data, but no system is completely secure.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot means you accept any changes.

## 10. Contact
For questions or data requests, contact zekkie at zekkiewashere[at]icloud[dot]com.
